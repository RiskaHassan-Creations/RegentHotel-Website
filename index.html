About Regent...


Welcome to Regent,where every burger tells a story of passion,quality and creativity.Nestled in heart of Kandy,our restaurant was born out of a simple yet powerful idea:to elevate the classic burger experience.From the moment you step through our doors, you'll find yourself immersed in a culinary journey that celebrates the finest ingredients, innovative flavors and a commitment to perfection.

At Regent, we believe that a great burger is more than just a meal it's an experience.That's why we source the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, crafting each burger with care and precision. Our chefs are not just cooks but artists, constantly experimenting with bold combinations and unique twists to create signature burgers that you won't find anywhere else.But we're more than just about great food. We're about community, connection and making every guest feel like family. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our warm, welcoming atmosphere invites you to relax and savor every moment.

Join us at Regent, where flavor meets creativity and every bite is a celebration of what a burger can truly be. We can't wait to share our love for burgers with you...
